Dea. Joseph HAYWARD, 8th child of Thomas HAYWARD and Susanna TOWNE [Family], was born about 1646 in Duxbury, Plymouth Colony. Joseph died on 06/20/1718 in Bridgewater, Plymouth County, Province Of Massachusetts Bay aged about 72.
