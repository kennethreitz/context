1. == Biography ==Christopher Hepler was born in 1777 in Lehigh, Pennsylvania, United States. He was the son of [[Hepler-138|Casper Hepler Junior]] and [[Schmidt-5815|Anna Maria (Schmidt) Hepler]]. He married Catharina (Wagner)Hepler on October 31, 1799 in Berks, Pennsylvania, United States. Together, they were the parents of Benjamin Wagner Hepler (1800 - 1869), Magdalena (Hepler) Geist (1803 - 1869), Catharine (Hepler) Haas (1805 - 1892), Anna Maria (Hepler) Howerter (1808 - 1876), George WashingtonHepler (1810 - 1848), Elizabeth (Hepler) Geist (1813 - 1888), Daniel Wagner Hepler (1815 - 1894), John Wagner Hepler (1818 - 1898), and Joel Wagner Hepler (1825 - 1860). Christopher Hepler passed away in 1847 in Upper Mahantongo (area now part of Eldred Township) Schuylkill, Pennsylvania, United States. He was buried in the Zions Evangelical Congregational Church Cemetery in Pitman, Schuylkill, Pennsylvania, United States. == Sources == <references />* [https://www.findagrave.com/memorial/34498458/christopher-hepler Find A Grave Memorial# 34498458: Christopher Hepler]
