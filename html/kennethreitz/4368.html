1. == Biography == Levi Comstock a proven Loyalist lived at Leeds; stepson to Ephraim Eyers, also a Loyalist Levi's wife, Abigail, was stepdaughter of Charles Griffin, a UEL. Levi's sisters: Sylvia Comstock, who married William Merrick, founder of Merrickville & Lavinia Comstock, who married Capt. Thomas Smythe, founder of Smith's Falls and also had son, Terrence, whose wife wasrelated to Benedict Arnold.
