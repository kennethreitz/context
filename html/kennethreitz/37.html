Colonel Daniel AXTELL, 5th child of William AXTELL and Thomasine CUTLER [Family], was born on 05/26/1622 in Great Berkhampstead, Hertfordshire, England. Daniel died on 10/19/1660 in Hung, Drawn And Quartered Intyburn, Middlesex, England aged 38.
