Johann "George" HEPLER, 8th child of Johann HEPLER and Anna SCHMIDT [Family], was born on 06/22/1790 in Lehigh, Pennsylvania, United States. George died on 02/24/1867 in Schuylkill County Pennsylvania aged 76.

== Biography ==Johann Georg "George" Hepler was born in 1790 in Upper Milford Township, Northampton, Pennsylvania, United States now Lehigh, Pennsylvania,United States. He was the son of [[Hepler-138|Johan Casper Hepler Junior]] and [[Schmidt-5815|Anna Maria (Schmidt) Hepler]]. He married Salome (Beisel) Hepler in 1810. Together, they were the parents of Jacob Beisel Hepler (1813 - 1883), Salome (Hepler) Herb (1816 - 1856), Rachel (Hepler) Maurer (1821 - 1906), George Beisel Hepler (1823 - 1892), and Hannah (Hepler) Geist (1831 - 1873). His first wife passed away in 1849. He married Elizabeth Reiner Yoder after 1849. He passed away in 1867 in Eldred Township, Schuylkill, Pennsylvania, United States. He was buried in Zions Evangelical Congregational Church Cemetery in Pitman, Schuylkill, Pennsylvania, United States. == Sources ==. <references />* [https://www.findagrave.com/memorial/34503026/johann-georg-hepler Find A Grave Memorial #34503026: Johann Georg Hepler].
