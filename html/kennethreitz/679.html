Sebastian BROSIUS1, eldest child of Johan George BROSIUS and Anna Catherine SIMON, was born about 1725 in Weierbach, Rheinland, Germany. Sebastian died about 01/03/1791 in Upper Mahoney, Northumberland, Pennsylvania, USA aged about 66.

Margaretha ROTH2, only child of Joseph ROTH and Unknown UNKNOWN, was born in 1729 in Rehrersburg, Berks, Province Of Pennsylvania. She died about 05/18/1789 in Upper Mahoney, Northumberland, Pennsylvania, USA aged about 60.

On 11/29/1748 Sebastian married Margaretha. 3 daughters given.
