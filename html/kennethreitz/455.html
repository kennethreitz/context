Johann REITZ Wrights-Oh, 2nd child of Andreas REITZ and Maria-Margaretha "Anna" BROSIUS [Family], was born on 03/12/1785 in Northumberland County, Pennsylvania. Johann died on 02/03/1865 in Bethel Twp., Miami County, Ohio aged 79.
