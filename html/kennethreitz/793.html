Hans Adam SIMON1, eldest child of Johann Peter SIMON and Maria KIEFFER, was born on 11/30/1664 in Herren-Sulzbach, Rheinland-Pfalz, Germany. Hans died in 1712 in Sulzbach, Aichach-Friedberg, Bayern, Germany aged about 48.

Anna Catherine HOEHN2, only child of Johann Thomas HOEHN and Anna Amalia NICLAUS, was born on 08/16/1674 in Herrensulzbach, Rheinland, Germany. She died in 1692 in Herren-Sulzbach, Rhineland, Prussia aged about 18.

On 04/27/1688 Hans married Anna. One son and 4 daughters given.
