[[Category:Puritan_Great_Migration]] {{Puritan Great Migration|needs=Research|needs1=Biography}} == Biography == :Mary Brownson was baptized December 12, 1627 at Earls Colne, Essex, England.<ref> Baptism register 1620 to 1639 (D/P209/1/2) 12.10.1627 (Friday 12 October 1627) document 6702624 "Mary daughter of Jn Brownson and Fran Brownson christened 12.10.1627" </ref>the daughter of John Bronson<ref name=Sibley>[https://archive.org/stream/bronsonlineage1600sibl#page/11/mode/1up| Sibley, Harriet Bronson. ''"Bronson Lineage"'' 1636-1917] Dallas, Oregon. 1917, p. 11</ref> In 1635 she accompanied her parents to Massachussets, probablyon the "Defense" with other people from Earls Colne. She grew up in Hartford and married John Wyatt by 1647, John Graves perhaps in 1671, William Allis in 1679 and Samuel Gaylord in 1681. She died after 1694. Death:: She was living in 1694.<ref> Jacobus, Donald Lines . The ancestry of Lorenzo Ackley & his wife Emma Arabella Bosworth / compiled and published by N. Grier Parke, II; (Woodstock, Vt. : Elm Tree Press, 1960.)</ref> Jacobus does not state why he knows this, but somewhere there isa document signed or acknowledged by her in 1694. : After 22 SEP 1689 (the date of husband Samuel's will) :Find a grave says she died 1688, but she was alive still then.:Apparent incorrect report of being killed by Indians in the HatfieldMassacre on September 19, 1677. Her husband John Graves was killed in the massacre.
