Benoni MOORE1, 2nd child of Joseph MOORE and Lydia "Lydiah" AXTELL, was born on 04/14/1669 in Sudbury, Middlesex, Massachusetts Bay. Benoni died on 11/18/1753 in Northfield, Franklin, Massachusetts Bay aged 84.

Mehitable ALLIS2, eldest child of Samuel ALLIS and Alice UNKNOWN, was born on 07/02/1677 in Hatfield, Hampshire, Massachusetts Bay. She died on 05/08/1757 in Northfield, Franklin, Massachusetts Bay aged 79.

On 12/13/1698 Benoni married Mehitable. 3 sons and 7 daughters given.
