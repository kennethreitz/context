Henry IDDINGS1, only child of William IDDINGS and Mary MOORE, was born in 1727 in Nantmeal, Chester, Pennsylvania. Henry died on 11/21/1819 in Buffalo Twp, Union, Pennsylvania aged about 92.

Mary WYNNE2, eldest daughter of Jonathan WYNNE and Anne WARNER, was born on 11/19/1733 in E Nantmeal, Chester, Pennsylvania. She died about 1778 in Mazeppa, Union, Pennsylvania aged about 45.

On 03/23/1752 Henry married Mary. 3 sons and one daughter given.
