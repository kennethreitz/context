Notes

1. [[Category: Raid on Deerfield]] == Biography ==Daughter of John Hawks (1643 - c 1722) & Alice Hawks. She was taken captive during the French & Indian attack on Deerfield, the "Deerfield Massacre" on 29 Feb 1703/4. She was slain en route to Montreal, Canada.
