Thomas MERRICK1, 5th child of Thomas MERRICK and Elizabeth TILLEY, was born on 04/12/1664 in Springfield, Hampden, Massachusetts. Thomas died on 08/16/1743 in Springfield, Hampden, Massachusetts aged 79.

Hannah DUMBLETON2, 10th child of John DUMBLETON and Mercy MARSHFIELD, was born in 1670 in Springfield, Hampshire, Massachusetts Bay. She died on 10/25/1737 in Springfield, Hampshire, Massachusetts Bay aged about 67.

On 12/18/1690 Thomas married Hannah. 3 sons and 2 daughters given.
