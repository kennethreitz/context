1. == Biography == Jacob Moore was born on 28 Apr, 1645, in Sudbury, Massachusetts. He was the son of John and Elizabeth (Rice) Moore. Jacob married ElizabethLoker on 29 May, 1667, in Sudbury, Massachusetts. Jacob and his wife,Elizabeth, had 12 children during their marriage. He died on 23 Mar, 1716, in his hometown of Sudbury, Massachusetts. ==Sources== <references /> *''Vital Records of Sudbury, Massachusetts, until 1850'' * "Some Descendants of John Moore of Sudbury, Mass."
