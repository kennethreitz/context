William ALLIS, 3rd child of Samuel ALLIS and Alice UNKNOWN [Family], was born on 10/19/1680 in Hatfield, Hampshire County, Massachusetts Bay Colony. William died about 02/20/1763 in Montague, Franklin County, Province Of Massachusetts Bay aged 82.
