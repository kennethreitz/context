Levi COMSTOCK UE was the eldest child of Unknown COMSTOCK [Family]. Levi died in 1804.

== Biography ==. Levi Comstock a proven Loyalist lived at Leeds; stepson to Ephraim Eyers, also a Loyalist. Levi's wife, Abigail, was stepdaughter of Charles Griffin, a UEL. Levi's sisters: Sylvia Comstock, who married William Merrick, founder of Merrickville & Lavinia Comstock, who married Capt. Thomas Smythe, founder of Smith's Falls and also had son, Terrence, whose wife wasrelated to Benedict Arnold. Levi's brother: Aaron Comstock, marr Phoebe Elliot of Leeds.
