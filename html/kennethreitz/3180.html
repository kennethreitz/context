1. ---- == Biography == Charles^ , son of (Ebenezer^, TJiomas^, Thomas'^) and Mehitable (Moore) Merrick, born 1740, at Cazenovia, N. Y. Married, 1764, Susanna Lawrence, of Stillwater, N. Y. Underwood's History of the Pollard Family says: "Charles and Susanna Merrick removed to Saratoga, N. Y., where they owned a farm known as the "deer lick," comprising the present site of the Springs.
